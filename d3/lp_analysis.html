Who has the risk to become a low performer? Does, for example, income or immigration background matters?
Further analysis based on weighted logistic regression considers influence of some potential risk factors in different countries. Values in a heatmap correspond to logistic coefficient and represent potential change in logarithm of odd ratio between group of low performers and others. Note that positive sign means increase in probability to become a low performer.

Considered factors make the following comparisons: GENDER: “Female” vs “Male”, PRIMED: “No primary education” vs “At least one year of primary education”, ESCS_GR: “Disadvantaged social and economic status” vs “Medium and advantaged social and economic status”, FAMSTRUC: “Single parent family” vs “Two parent family”, IMMIG: “Immigrant” vs “Native”, SCHTYPE: “Public” vs “Private”, SCHLOC: “Rural” vs “Urban”, DIFFLNG: “Speak different language” vs “Speak the same language”.
